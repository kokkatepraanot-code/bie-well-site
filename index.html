About Joseph — in his own words

Meet Joseph Bielefeld, PMHNP-BC, FNP-BC

I have over 15 years of experience working in emergency services as well as inpatient and outpatient psychiatry. Throughout my career, I have come to recognize that mental health extends beyond medication alone; it encompasses nutrition, sleep hygiene, physical activity, and meaningful connection with friends, family, and nature.

My background in psychology allows me to better understand individual behaviors and provide thoughtful, practical solutions to life's ongoing challenges. I strive to create a safe, supportive, and nonjudgmental environment where clients feel heard, respected, and empowered. I welcome open dialogue and am fully committed to maintaining the highest standards of client confidentiality.

What to expect

What to expect during our sessions

FIRST VISIT

A thorough evaluation, not a rushed intake

Initial visits include a thorough psychiatric evaluation and detailed medical history to support accurate diagnosis and individualized treatment planning. Care is guided by the latest evidence-based standards, including diagnostic criteria from the American Psychiatric Association's DSM-5-TR.

TREATMENT

Careful psychopharmacology and psychoeducation

Treatment is provided for a range of conditions, including bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, psychosis, paranoia, and delusional disorders. Sessions emphasize psychopharmacology, extensive psychoeducation, and close monitoring for medication efficacy and potential side effects, including extrapyramidal symptoms (EPS).

FOLLOW-UP

Ongoing monitoring and adjustment

Follow-up visits focus on mood, anxiety, life stressors, medication adjustments, and overall treatment satisfaction. Ongoing care includes targeted support for motivation, insomnia, anxiety, thought processes, and functional barriers impacting daily living, learning, and personal development.

THE GOAL

Stability, functioning, and long-term outcomes

The goal of care is to promote stability, improve functioning, and support long-term mental health outcomes through a structured, patient-centered approach.
